EECS Facilities

The Department of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science maintains an advanced collection of a laboratory resources for students. These ever expanding laboratories are funded by private donations, student engineering fees, and department funds.

About Eaton Hall

Eaton Hall houses the dean's office suite; the Department of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science; the Engineering Career Services Center; classrooms; hardware labs and computer labs; an atrium and computing commons; faculty and graduate teaching assistant offices; and the multimedia Spahr Engineering Classroom, which seats 250.
